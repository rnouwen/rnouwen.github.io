#  Dr. Rick W.F. Nouwen

born September 12, 1974, Heerlen, the Netherlands
 
current position: Associate Professor (Dutch: "Universitair Hoofddocent"), Department of Languages Literature & Communication, Humanities Faculty, Utrecht University

## Career 

- **2014 -**, Associate Professor, Linguistics/AI, Humanities Faculty, Utrecht University
- **2011**, Post-doctoral associate, Linguistics and Philosophy, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Cambridge, US
- **2008-2013**, Postdoc/Assistant Professor, Principle Investigator NWO VIDI, Linguistics/AI, Humanities, Utrecht University
- **2008-2011**, Docent, Communication and Information Sciences, Humanities Faculty, Utrecht University
- **2005-2008**, Postdoc, Principle Investigator NWO VENI, Humanities Faculty, Utrecht University
- **2004-2005**, Postdoc, Graduiertenkolleg Satzarten, J.W. Goethe Universitaet Frankfurt/Main, Germany
- **2003-2004**, Postdoc, Niels Stensen project, School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh, Scotland
- **1999-2003**, PhD candidate, Utrecht Institute for Linguistics, Promotores: H. de Swart & J. van Eijck, defended 21-11-2003
